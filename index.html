About Me

I'm Mihir Savla, a Linux & server administration enthusiast with hands-on experience across enterprise hosting environments. Currently working as a Product Support Associate at Newfold Digital since May 2023, where I manage and troubleshoot Linux-based shared/VPS servers for premium customers.
